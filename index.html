Andrei Soltan creates software. He likes working on big systems that have to be always on.

Today, Andrei is a software development engineer at Amazon, where he enjoys working on Fulfillment by Amazon. In the past, he has been a teaching assistant, jack-of-all-trades junior developer, and student at the University of Toronto, where he completed a degree in Computer Science.

Having travelled and lived in different corners of the world, Andrei can hold a conversation in English, Romanian and Russian, can order food and watch a movie in French, and find his way around a city in German and Japanese.

He is currently in Toronto, where he enjoys the Canadian hospitality, snow and maple syrup.

In his free time, Andrei likes to read books and solve puzzles ranging from escape-the-room to video games.
